Meet one of our critics.

Davey Huang

Interlochen, MI

Originally from Taipei, Taiwan, Davey Huang is a Motion Picture Arts major studying at Interlochen Arts Academy in Interlochen, Michigan. He started becoming interested in filmmaking since the age of six, and started my pursue of the craft of filmmaking since the summer of 2014. Huang’s favorite film is Federico Fellini’s 8 1/2. He is most interested in the aspects of directing, editing, cinematography, sound mixing and visual effects, and has been on multiple sets during the study at Interlochen.
