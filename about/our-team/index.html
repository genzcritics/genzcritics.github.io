Our Team

Amateur film critic Elizabeth Kim and New York Film Critics Series Producer Mark Ehrenkranz founded Gen Z Critics in 2016 when they realized the lack of emphasis on youth in the world of film criticism.

Elizabeth Kim

Co-founder

A freshman at Stanford University, Elizabeth “Betsy” Kim was the head of her high school school film club and intern for the Boston Society of Film Critics. Her favorite movies include Lady Bird and Dunkirk.

Mark Ehrenkranz

Co-founder

Mark Ehrenkranz is the producer of the New York Film Critics Series. He has developed comedy videos for Jerry Seinfeld, Jay Leno, and Whoopi Goldberg, in addition to having collaborated with other theatrical NYC comedians.

Jami Ganz

Social media consultant

Jami Ganz is pursuing an MSc in Media and Communications at London School of Economics. Previously, she was a writer at Entertainment Weekly. Some of her favorite movies include Lost in Translation, Her, and Atonement.

Sharon Kim

Web developer

Sharon Kim is a recent graduate of Wellesley College, where she pursued a B.A. in Computer Science with a minor in music. Her favorite movies are Amélie, The Grand Budapest Hotel, and La La Land.
