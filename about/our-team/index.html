Our Team

College student Elizabeth Kim and New York Film Critics Series Producer Mark Ehrenkranz founded Gen Z Critics in 2016 when they realized the lack of youth opinion in film criticism.

Elizabeth Kim

Co-founder

A freshman at Stanford University, Elizabeth “Betsy” Kim interned at the Cannes Film Festival in 2018. Her favorite movies of 2018 are Cold War, Annihilation, and Widows.

Mark Ehrenkranz

Co-founder

Mark Ehrenkranz is the producer of the New York Film Critics Series. He has developed comedy videos for Jerry Seinfeld, Jay Leno, and Whoopi Goldberg, in addition to having collaborated with other theatrical NYC comedians.

Jami Ganz

Social media consultant

Jami Ganz is a recent graduate of the London School of Economics, where she pursued an MSc in Media and Communications. Previously, she was a writer at Entertainment Weekly. Some of her favorite movies include Lost in Translation, Her, and Atonement.

Sharon Kim

Web developer

Sharon Kim is a recent graduate of Wellesley College, where she pursued a B.A. in Computer Science with a minor in music. Her favorite movies are Amélie, The Grand Budapest Hotel, La La Land, and the Hayao Miyazaki movies.
